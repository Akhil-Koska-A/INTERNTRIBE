Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji, also known as Fuji-san, is the highest mountain in Japan, standing at an elevation of 3,776 meters. It is an active stratovolcano located on the Japanese island of Honshu, about 100 km southwest of Tokyo, and is visible from several places in Tokyo on a clear day.
